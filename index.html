Overview

Dr Lal PathLabs diagnostic centers provide a wide range of health screening solutions along with value added services that include state-of-the-art pathology, radiology and cardiology services. In today’s hectic lifestyle where stress breeds many silent diseases, a thorough checkup is the easiest way to detect and fight diseases before it is too late. The Health Packages from Dr Lal PathLabs are specifically designed keeping in mind that not all diseases produce early warning signs and aim to provide accurate detection of such diseases.

At DLPL, we provide quality and safe preventive and curative testing services to employees in corporates across India. Apart from our health body check-ups, we specialize in conducting drug tests, food handler check-ups, and any hazardous and Covid related check-ups as well. We cover a 360-degree facility by conducting everything from annual to pre- employment checkups. We enroll companies that encourage seamless testing facilities for employees and their families. Our highly trained and efficient staff perform onsite and offsite checkups by extending customized packages to fulfill customer needs.
